Hello my Dear Reader!

I am Yin Minn Pa Pa, originally from Myanmar and currently working at Japan as security researcher. I finished Ph.D in computer engineering from Yokohama National University, Japan.This site is made to document my professional life and share it with you. I will try to write most of my blog posts in Burmese language so that Burmese learners can learn security easily.

You can find list of my research publications here.

Here is brief about me.

My research interests:

  • Malware Analysis
  • IoT security
  • Web security
  • Wireless Security
